War Thunder — Game Review

Realistic Military Combat • Vehicles • Combined Arms

Overall Review

Multiplayer Realistic Combat 8.7 / 10

War Thunder is a large-scale vehicular combat game featuring aircraft, tanks, helicopters, and warships across multiple eras. With detailed physics, realistic damage models, and a massive tech tree, the game offers one of the most complete military combat simulations available today.

Key Features

  • Realistic physics-based combat
  • Hundreds of planes, tanks, and warships
  • Large-scale combined arms battles
  • Constant updates and new content

Pros

  • Highly detailed vehicle models
  • Fun progression system
  • Massive variety of vehicles

Cons

  • High grind at later tiers
  • Some balance issues
